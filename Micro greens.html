"MICROGREENS"

microgreens Microgreens are the young shoots of vegetables, herbs, or flowers, harvested shortly after germination, typically within 7 to 21 days.
Microgreens farming is a profitable and space-efficient agricultural practice that involves growing young edible greens packed with nutrients, making it an attractive option for both small-scale and commercial farming.

Benefits:

  • May improve heart health by reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease.
  • May help fight cancer.
  • May have anti-diabetic properties.
  • May reduce kidney disease risk.
  • Can lower blood pressure.
